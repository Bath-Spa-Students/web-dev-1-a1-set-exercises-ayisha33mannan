Storyline/Summaries

Kamala, a superhero fan with an imagination--particularly when it comes to Captain Marvel--feels like she doesn't fit in at school and sometimes even at home, that is until she gets superpowers like the heroes she admires.

Plot  of   Ms Marvel

Kamala Khan, a Pakistani American teenager and Avengers fangirl, gains superpowers from a mysterious bangle given to her by her grandmother. With her new shape-shifting abilities, Kamala decides to become a superhero and protect her city as Ms. Marvel.

Kamala's parents and the Department of Damage Control (DODC) oppose her superhero activities, fearing for her safety. However, Kamala befriends Bruno Carrelli, a tech-savvy ally, and Nakia Bahadir, a social activist who supports her desire to help others.

As Ms. Marvel, Kamala faces various villains and challenges, including a group of rogue Inhumans and a DODC agent who is secretly working for a villainous organization. Along the way, Kamala learns about her past and heritage, and discovers that her powers are connected to a larger story involving the Inhumans.

In the final battle, Kamala teams up with her allies to defeat the villains and save her city.

  • 🗝
