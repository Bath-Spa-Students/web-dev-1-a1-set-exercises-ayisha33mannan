Storyline/Summaries

Trivia

  • Iman Vellani became a fan of Kamala Khan/Ms. Marvel when she read the 19 issue run of Ms. Marvel Mecca, mirroring Kamala's fan-girl tendencies by dressing up as Ms.Marvel for Halloween when she was 15.

  • The S in the Ms Marvel logo is in the shape of an Arabic "kaaf" (used as the letter "K" in Arabic) which eventually becomes the symbol on her costume.
